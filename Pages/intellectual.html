Intellectual Hobbies

Reading:

My granny used to encourage me to read when I was young and I mostly read fiction back then. When I met my husband I began reading more philosophy and theology. I have loved reading poetry since high school and still enjoy reading poetry.

Writing Poetry:

I loved reading and writing poetry in school. I still write poetry on the odd occassion and have recently sent one of my poems to be reviewed by a poetry journal called New Contrast! If they like the poem they will publish it in their next issue.
